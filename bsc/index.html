ABOUT THE $RICE TOKEN

RICE AI is a decentralized AI foundry powered by Rice Robotics. It aims to solve the AI robotics data scarcity bottleneck by building a reward system for robotics data sharing on top of a DePIN layer, with $RICE as its main utility token.

Individuals or companies can contribute robotics data to the RICE AI foundry in exchange for token rewards. The collected data will be sold to research labs and companies in need. 

In-house robotics foundation models are also trained using the data collected from the foundry and will be accessible for robotics companies or robot owners on a monthly subscription model. If clients pay with our native token, a discount will be offered. 

$RICE is designed to be deflationary in the long run. A portion of the fee collected from data purchasers will be used to burn tokens, thereby reducing the overall supply. 

The token is also used as the governance token for the data collection protocol. More utility will be added along with the platform development.
