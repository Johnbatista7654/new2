The Launch Pool will begin on August 27, 2025 at 16:00 UTC and remain open for 48 hours, ending on August 29 at 16:00 UTC.

A Launch Pool is a token sale mechanism designed for organic price discovery and limited sniping or front running. Here's how it works:

A specific quantity of tokens is allocated to the Launch Pool contract. The Launch Pool will remain open for a set period of time.

While the Launch Pool contract remains open, users can deposit SOL into it or withdraw their SOL from it.

When the Launch Pool ends, tokens are distributed proportionally based on each user's share of the total deposits.

There will also be an early deposit multiplier to reward early participation and a scaling withdrawal penalty to disincentivize late withdrawals.

Hours 0–24: Flat +25% early deposit boost (timezone-friendly).

Hours 24–36: Bonus linearly tapers from +25% → 0%.

Hours 36–48: Withdrawal fee linearly ramps from 0% → 25%.

(So hours 24–36 have some bonus; hours 36–48 have no bonus and an rising exit fee.)

After the Launch Pool ends, all participants will see their pro rata share of $CARDS based on the amount of SOL deposited factoring in any early deposit multipliers.

All purchasers will be able to claim their share of $CARDS 3 hours after the Launch Pool ends (August 29th at 19:00 UTC). Come back to this site to claim.

No lock-up period.

Only SOL will be accepted as deposits into the Launch Pool.
